Time Strings

Time periods are typically relative to some other moment or event.
For example, when defining a "restock" time period for a shop, a period of "2 hours" would be 2 hours after the last time restocked, and Mob Respawns would be relative to whenever the mob last died, etc.

Time Periods

You can add "irl" or "real" keywords to use real time instead of in-game time.
For Example: "1 real hour", or "2 weeks irl"

Special Periods

These are specific in-game moments in time that are not relative to any other time period.
  • sunrise
  • sunset
  • midnight
  • noon
